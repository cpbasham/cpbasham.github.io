Thinking Style and Learning

Perception & Ordering Ability

6/11/15

Concrete Perception enables you to register information directly through your five senses: sight, smell, touch, taste, and hearing. When you are using your concrete ability, you are dealing with the obvious, the "here and now." You are not looking for hidden meanings, or making relationships between ideas or concepts. "It is what it is."

Sequential Ordering Ability allows your mind to organize information in a linear, step-by-step manner. When using your sequential ability, you are following a logical train of thought, a traditional approach to dealing with information. You may also prefer to have a plan and to follow it, rather than relying on impulse.

My struggles so far at DBC have mostly been related to time management and allocation. I definitely have a concrete sequential thinking/learning style, which manifests mostly in my desire to focus on the programming aspects of DBC and to get through each step quickly. I want to learn Ruby sequentially like everyone else, but I also already know how to program so I want to take these steps in strides, rather than slowly get through the material as we also do things like write blog posts. I feel like it's slowing me down because I generally like to take on one big project and just tackle it as quickly as possible.
